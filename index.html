About Us

Leading software development and tech company.

CEO, Keith Guda

Enagel Solutions, founded in 2021 by Keith Guda and a team of visionary engineers, emerged from a shared commitment to bridging gaps in the technology landscape. With a strong foundation built on collaboration and deep technical expertise, the company quickly established itself as a trusted provider of innovative software solutions. From crafting sophisticated websites to developing cutting-edge AI and machine learning applications, and creating robust mobile apps, Enagel Solutions has consistently delivered excellence. Our team’s dedication to quality and innovation drives us as we continue to shape the future of technology with confidence and precision.

At Enagel Solutions, our motto is simple yet profound: "Empowering Dreams, Crafting Excellence." Our mission is to deliver fast, efficient, and high-quality software solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. We aim to expand our reach and employment opportunities globally while continuing to invite skilled, passionate, and motivated developers, marketers, researchers, managers, and accountants in Kenya to join us in driving innovation and growth.
